Value-Added Services, curated to exceed the boundaries of basic voice and data communications

STL's Value-Added services encompass a wide variety of offerings, including entertainment, information, and utility services. Our VAS services enhance user experience beyond basic voice and data communication.

These services provide additional features and functionality to subscribers which include Bulk SMS services, Mobile Banking Apps, Content Services, Location-Based Services, Mobile Advertising and Mobile Health Services using the USSD channel.

STL's telecoms services offer susbcribers on the mobile networks a wide range of value-added services provided by third-party content and service providers within the telecommunications ecosystem.

Service Aggregation: We aggregate various VAS offerings, such as SMS-based services, mobile apps, entertainment content, gaming and more, from multiple providers into a single platform.

Content Management: STL manages the content and services offered to subscribers, ensuring quality, compliance with regulations, and compatibility with the mobile operator networks in Nigeria.

Billing and Revenue Sharing: We manage the collection of fees from subscribers and distribute revenue among content providers and telecom operators based on pre-negotiated agreements.

API Integration: We provide application programming interfaces (APIs) that enable seamless integration of third-party services with the telecom network. This integration allows for the delivery of VAS to subscribers in real-time.

Content Discovery: STL assist subscribers in discovering and accessing VAS offerings through various channels, such as SMS, USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data), mobile apps or web portals.
